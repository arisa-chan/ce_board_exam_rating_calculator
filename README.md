# CE Board Exam Rating Calculator
A simple application to calculate your rating in Philippine civil engineering licensure examination based on your scores. Suitable for people who are currently reviewing or has just finished the board exam.

Available for Windows, MacOS and Linux. Head over to the **Releases** page to download a copy.
